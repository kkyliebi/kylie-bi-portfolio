# Audi Integrated Brand Communication — Project Archaeology

> Review status: first editorial excavation pass based on the current canonical record and confirmed portfolio positioning. This document identifies the questions needed to move the case from a broad experience summary to a credible, specific professional case study.

## Current Strength

Audi is the portfolio’s strongest evidence of real-world scale, cross-functional collaboration, luxury communication, and the ability to connect concept with production and execution.

Its deepest value is likely not any single visual asset. It is Kylie’s role as a bridge across client needs, creative development, production constraints, and final delivery.
