## Project Overview

**Madam, in Eden, I'm Adam** is a visual narrative and photography project built around staged imagery, sequencing, and controlled post-production. It explores how a photographic series can communicate through gesture, repetition, visual tension, and the relationship between individual frames.

The project is relevant to the portfolio because it shows the development of a concept into a complete visual narrative rather than a collection of unrelated photographs.

The project was developed through a planned visual sequence. The concept relied on the relationship between images, the use of the hand as a recurring performative element, and the construction of a consistent atmosphere across the series.
